# GovCon Premium Content Agent Positioning

This release note clarifies how the Premium Content Agent should
position SourceDeck when drafting GovCon marketing/content.

## Required positioning line

SourceDeck helps small businesses organize GovCon pursuit workflows,
prepare review-ready outreach/content, and manage capture activity with
human approval at every decision point.

## GovCon post prompt rules

- Use the required positioning line as the base positioning sentence
  when drafting GovCon posts.
- Adapt only for platform length; preserve workflow organization,
  review-ready drafts, capture activity management, and human approval.
- Keep outputs as draft-only content preparation.
- Do not claim SourceDeck wins contracts.
- Do not claim guaranteed awards, revenue, compliance, or outreach
  success.
- Do not claim auto-send, auto-post, or autonomous GovCon
  decision-making.

## Content safety and approval workflow

The Premium Content Agent prepares draft captions, hooks, hashtags,
image prompts, carousel outlines, and claim-review checklists. The user
reviews, edits, approves, and posts manually. Human approval remains
required at every decision point.

## Example prompt

Draft a LinkedIn GovCon credibility post using this base sentence:
"SourceDeck helps small businesses organize GovCon pursuit workflows,
prepare review-ready outreach/content, and manage capture activity with
human approval at every decision point." Keep the draft review-ready,
avoid outcome guarantees, and label candidate claims for human review.

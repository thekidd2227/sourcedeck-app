# SourceDeck — Buyer Overview

## What SourceDeck does

SourceDeck is an operator-driven desktop workspace that helps small business teams organize the work around federal and commercial opportunities. It centralizes the inputs an operator already maintains — capability statement details, opportunity notes, outreach drafts, and partner research — and structures them into reviewable artifacts. Operators stay in control of every output.

## Who it is for

SourceDeck is built for GovCon-leaning small and mid-size businesses, capture and business development teams, and solo operators who run their own pipeline. It assumes a hands-on user who reviews and approves every artifact before it leaves the desk. Teams that want a fully automated send-and-forget system are not the target fit.

## What it helps organize

SourceDeck helps operators organize a single operating profile and capability statement, intake notes for opportunities, outreach drafts to prospective contacts, research on prime partners, and drafts of premium content. Every artifact is treated as a draft until a human approves it. The tool structures the work; the operator owns the decisions.

## What it does not do

SourceDeck does not guarantee contract awards, revenue, or pipeline outcomes. It does not submit proposals automatically, send outreach automatically, or provide unlimited AI usage. It is not a compliance certification and does not substitute for legal, contracting, or security review.

## AI provider options

Standard plans use customer-provided AI keys. Premium and enterprise deployments may include SourceDeck-managed IBM watsonx configuration or customer-provided AI credentials depending on workflow risk, usage volume, and deployment requirements. Usage limits, overages, or enterprise deployment terms may apply.

## Human approval model

Human approval remains required for outreach, proposal, pricing, compliance, bid/no-bid, teaming, publishing, and sending. Nothing is sent automatically. Drafts surface in the workspace for the operator to read, edit, approve, or discard before any external action takes place.

## Premium deployment language

Premium and enterprise deployments may add SourceDeck-managed AI provisioning, additional configuration work, and tailored operator onboarding. Scope, timing, and pricing for those engagements are described in the separate pricing and packaging materials. No premium capability is implied beyond what those materials state.

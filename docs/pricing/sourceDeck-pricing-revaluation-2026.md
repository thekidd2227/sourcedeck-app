## Why SourceDeck now justifies higher pricing

SourceDeck is no longer a SAM.gov search wrapper. The current product
includes a connected GovCon capture + outreach + content operating
system:

- **GovCon Capture Suite** — deadline extraction, subcontractor
  sourcing, incumbent research, scheduled SAM search, deterministic
  solicitation analysis, proposal workspace, compliant Q&A drafting,
  exports.
- **SAM.gov Opportunity Outreach Agent** — closing-window scanning,
  staged (never sent) outreach drafts with approval gates.
- **Prime Partner Finder** — NAICS-based prime matching, scoring,
  capability memos, draft outreach (no auto-send).
- **GovCon Operating Profile Wizard** — full business / identifier /
  targeting / content / safety onboarding.
- **Capability statement extraction** — candidate-field extraction,
  user-approved.
- **AI provider onboarding** — OpenAI / Anthropic / watsonx (presence-only).
- **Creative / imaging provider onboarding** — Canva + placeholder
  (presence-only; manual image prompt workflow today).
- **Premium GovCon Content Agent** — draft-only LinkedIn / Meta /
  Facebook / Instagram / TikTok content from the operating profile.
- **Social platform content-prep context** — handles + default platforms
  for drafting (no posting).
- **Secure credential boundary** — keys never reach the renderer; exports
  strip secrets.
- **No-auto-send approval gates** — RED_RESTRICTED, irreversible KILL,
  human approval before outreach/posting.
- **Release hardening + smoke checks** — integration audit, RC gate,
  functional + manual smoke.

## Positioning notes

- One-time tiers anchor the implementation value; the monthly Operator
  tier captures ongoing AI + capture + content value.
- Do **not** claim compliance/certification (SOC 2, FedRAMP, CMMC, ISO,
  HIPAA, HITRUST, CUI) as part of pricing justification — none are
  verified.
- Do **not** claim platform publishing or auto-posting as a feature.
- watsonx and repo/creative connectors are positioned as planned premium
  architecture where not yet implemented.

## 8. Multi-office terms

- Each additional office (above the plan cap) adds $997/mo or operator-discretion annual equivalent.
- Office is defined as: a distinct Pursuit Profile + vendor partition + role-separation set, NOT a physical location. Customers with two physical locations sharing one Pursuit Profile do not need an additional-office charge.
- Multi-office customers should be sold on Enterprise 25 (1–3 offices included) or Enterprise Command (unlimited) rather than stacking Capture Team 10 + 5 additional offices.
- Multi-office governance (audit log retention, AI usage telemetry, role separation) is a standard tenant feature in Enterprise 25 and Enterprise Command; offering it à la carte for lower tiers requires ARCG approval.

## 9. Seat expansion terms

- Standard user add-on: $249/user/mo. Operator may pre-quote up to 5 standard users without escalation.
- Power user add-on: $399/user/mo. Operator may pre-quote up to 3 power users without escalation.
- Beyond those thresholds: operator escalates to ARCG to confirm pricing (likely path is upgrading the customer to the next plan tier).
- Annual seat expansions: prorated against the annual period; renew at the new seat count on the next anniversary.

## 10. Renewal note

| Trigger | Action |

| 30 days before renewal | Renewal quote sent; buyer signs or counter-proposes |

# SourceDeck Spanish Launch Marketing Handoff

**Prepared:** 2026-05-27
**Status:** Ready for marketing team pickup
**Note:** This doc lives in sourcedeck-app temporarily. Move to ARCGSystems repo when ready.

---

## 1. Release Summary

- SourceDeck now supports **English and Spanish**.
- English remains the default language.
- Spanish can be activated through a topbar language switcher or by appending `?lang=es` to the URL.
- 191 translated UI strings covering sidebar navigation, KPIs, buttons, form labels, settings, tooltips, and clinical/EHR labels.
- Credential migration completed — all AI provider keys now stored in OS-level encrypted storage (safeStorage). Privacy gate passed.
- 201/201 automated tests pass. 31/31 i18n audit checks pass.

---

## 2. Buyer Value

- **Spanish-speaking operators** can navigate the full SourceDeck interface in their language — sidebar, buttons, labels, settings, and validation states.
- **Bilingual GovCon and small-business teams** get better day-to-day usability without changing how SourceDeck works.
- **Broader adoption path** for teams with Spanish-speaking staff in operations, compliance, and vendor management roles.
- **Security upgrade shipped alongside** — credential handling modernized, privacy gate hardened.

---

## 3. Safe Claims

### Allowed
- "SourceDeck now supports English and Spanish."
- "Switch languages instantly with the built-in language selector."
- "English remains the default — Spanish is one click away."
- "191 interface elements translated: navigation, buttons, labels, tooltips, and settings."
- "Credential storage upgraded to OS-level encryption."
- "Privacy gate verified — no owner data in shipped builds."

### Avoid
- Do NOT claim full legal or compliance localization.
- Do NOT claim certified or official translation.
- Do NOT claim government or regulatory approval of the translation.
- Do NOT claim automatic multilingual proposal or document generation.
- Do NOT claim HIPAA/FedRAMP/SOC 2 compliance related to the translation.
- Do NOT claim "fully localized" — dynamic runtime data (KPI values, lead names, table rows) remains in English.
